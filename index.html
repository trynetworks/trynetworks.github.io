Features

Intuitive Design

Our intuitive design allows you to effortlessly browse, explore, and connect, all with the familiar user interface of your system.

Diverse Platform Support

Our platform supports some of the most popular Fediverse/ActivityPub platforms, including hundreds of servers. We currently support Mastodon, Pleroma, Pixelfed, Friendica, and Akkoma, with WriteFreely support coming soon.

Built-In Privacy

Our app is 100% private. All interactions connect directly between your device and the server. This means that we have no idea who you follow, which posts you've liked, and what you're posting.
